Best things to crochet when you're bored.

One of the best things to crochet is a scrunchie. It is easily made with a good use to it. also, you can make these in many different colors, and you can use them for handmade gifts for your friends or family.

Another good thing is a granny square. you can sew granny squares together to make a stylish cardigan, a cute tote bag, or a beutiful scarf!
