How to make a sandwich

You need these ingredients to make a sandwich.

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • Some kind of filling
  • Another filling (optional)

You can make a sandwich with these if you want

Chocolate Fruit Jam
Nutella Bananas Strawberry
Chocolate Chips Fresh Strawberrys Grape

Instructions:

  1. Put one filling onto one bread
  2. Put the other filling onto the other bread
  3. Put the two breads together
  4. Enjoy!
